Before we definitely end the game, you will play for 6 additional rounds. This time, you will be able to give reputation scores to each of the other players, depending on how they played. You will see the results of the last round after you read this page.

After investing in the public and private projects, you will see how the other people played. You will then give a score between 0 and 5 to each of the other players. A good score should be given to the players you think deserve a good reputation. The other players will also give you a reputation score.
